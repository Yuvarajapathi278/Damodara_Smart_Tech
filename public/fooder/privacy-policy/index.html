Privacy Policy

Fooder - Menu Scanner & Health Analysis

TL;DR: We collect minimal data to provide our menu scanning service. We don't sell your data, we use it only to improve your experience, and you can delete your account anytime.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Profile preferences (dietary restrictions, allergies, health goals)
  • Authentication data (managed by Supabase)

App Usage Data

  • Menu scan history (images are processed and not stored)
  • Health analysis preferences
  • App performance and crash reports
  • Device information (OS version, app version)

Menu Scanning Data

  • Photos of menus (processed by AI and immediately deleted)
  • Extracted menu data (dish names, ingredients, prices)
  • Health analysis results (stored temporarily for your review)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide our service: Process menu scans and generate health analysis
  • Improve your experience: Remember your preferences and dietary needs
  • Send updates: Notify you about new features or important changes
  • Ensure security: Prevent fraud and maintain platform integrity
  • Legal compliance: Meet legal requirements and resolve disputes

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (EU-based, GDPR compliant)
  • Groq: AI processing for menu analysis (privacy-first, data not retained)
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and crash reporting

Legal Requirements

We may share information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Menu photos are processed and immediately deleted
  • Access to your data is strictly controlled
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Scan history: Kept for 30 days, then automatically deleted
  • Menu photos: Never stored - processed and immediately discarded
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your personal data
  • Correction: Update your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data
  • Export: Download your data in portable format
  • Opt-out: Stop marketing communications

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of any material changes via email or app notification.
