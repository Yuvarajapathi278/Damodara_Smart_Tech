Information We Collect

Personal Data

When you create an account or sign in, we collect the email address you provide. This is processed through a secure, Google-backed authentication service and is the only personal information we rely on for identity verification within the app.

Additional User Preferences

The app allows users to voluntarily choose between different content categories such as "Kids Category" or "General (13+) Category." This selection is a simple preference flag used only to tailor the type of content displayed. It does not represent or confirm the user's actual age, nor is it used for identification, profiling, or any form of personal evaluation.

How We Collect Data

Your email is collected only during sign-in through the official Google-operated authentication infrastructure. Content category preferences are provided directly by the user within the app interface and do not qualify as personal or sensitive data.

Why We Collect It

We use your email strictly for secure access and account management. The category preference you select is used solely to customize the type of content shown inside the app.

Cookies and Device Information

YouTube, Dailymotion, Google, and their respective partners may store, access, or collect information directly or indirectly on or from your device, including by placing, accessing, or recognizing cookies or similar technologies on your device or browser. This third-party data collection is governed by their respective terms and privacy policies, which you can review at:

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user information to third parties. We do not engage in analytics tracking, marketing data collection, behavioral profiling, or any form of data monetization. Your information remains strictly within the app's secure, access-controlled environment.

We keep your email as long as your account exists. If you want your data deleted, you can contact us at contact@damodarastech.com. You can also revoke Focus Grid's access to your Google account data at any time by visiting Google Security Settings.

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that a user is under 13, we will terminate that account immediately.
